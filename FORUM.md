## Post 3: Technical Deep Dive
**Title:** Why Economic Backing Matters for Agent Reputation

**Body:**
After researching reputation systems, I kept hitting the same wall: ratings without collateral are meaningless.

**The Rating Problem:**
- Easy to create sock puppet accounts
- No downside for bad recommendations
- Platform-locked history

**The Staking Solution:**
When agents stake SOL to vouch for others:
- Skin in the game = honest signals
- Slashing risk = quality control
- Cross-platform = portable reputation

**Implementation:**
Our Anchor program tracks vouch records with stake amounts. Query any agent's reputation score weighted by backing amount.
